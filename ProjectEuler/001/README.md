### Algorithm

Subset Problem <br>
Answer = No of multiples of(3) + No of multiples of(5) - No of multiples(3*5) 

#### Summation of multiples of 3
- 3 + 6 + 9 + 12 +.....+ 3*i
- 3(1 + 2 + 3 + 4 +....+ i)
- 3/2(n)*(n+1)

#### Summation of multiples of 5
- 5 + 10 + 15 + 15 +.....+ 5*i
- 5(1 + 2 + 3 + 4 +....+ i)
- 5/2(n)*(n+1)

#### Summation of multiples of 3
- 15 + 30 + 45 + 60 +.....+ 15*i
- 3(1 + 2 + 3 + 4 +....+ i)
- 15/2(n)*(n+1)

```
Time Complexity : O(1)
Space Complexity : O(1)
```
